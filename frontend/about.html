Skincare Information

Light Diseases and Disorders of Pigmentation

  • Vitamin C Serums: Vitamin C is known for its brightening and antioxidant properties. It can help reduce the appearance of dark spots and improve overall skin tone..
  • Niacinamide (Vitamin B3): Niacinamide is effective in reducing hyperpigmentation and promoting an even skin tone. Look for serums or moisturizers containing this ingredient..

Lupus and other Connective Tissue diseases

  • Lupus and other connective tissue diseases can have various effects on the skin, and managing skin health is an important aspect of overall care. Skin symptoms in these conditions can include rashes, sensitivity, and increased sun sensitivity. Here are some skincare product recommendations that can help individuals with Lupus and other connective tissue diseases:
  • Gentle Cleansers: Use mild, fragrance-free c leansers to clean your skin without causing irritation. Look for cleansers that are labeled as "hypoallergenic" or "for sensitive skin."
  • Sunscreen: Sun protection is crucial for individuals with Lupus, as sunlight can trigger skin rashes and worsen symptoms. Choose a broad-spectrum sunscreen with at least SPF 30 and wear it daily, even on cloudy days."
